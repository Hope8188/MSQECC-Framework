# CSR Falsifiability Clause

## Frozen Clause

CSR is falsified if any of the following occur:

1. the anticommuting bridge fails to survive the many-body `T^3` extension as a nonzero local matter channel in the thermodynamic limit
2. no dimensionally consistent map from the proved bridge rate to a deterministic orbital kernel can be derived without introducing a new empirical scale by hand
3. the first fully derived frozen CSR galaxy law scores negative median per-galaxy `R^2` on an alias-resolved sample of at least `20` non-SPARC galaxies with published baryonic decompositions

This clause is public and should stay unchanged until one of the three items is met or failed directly.

## Why It Is Written This Way

The first clause is algebraic.

The second clause is mechanistic.

The third clause is empirical.

Together they force CSR to be a real physical programme rather than an interpretation layer that can absorb any outcome.

## Bottom Line

CSR is not validated by this clause yet.

It is also not protected from it.

That is the point.
