# Lumina App: The User Portal Guidelines (Liquid Sovereignty)

## 0. The Mission: Human-Centric Sovereignty
The **Lumina Horizon App** is the interface for the modern elite—users who demand the privacy of a vault with the fluidity of a high-end luxury watch. We move away from "scary" terminology toward **Human-Centric Sovereignty**.

## 1. Aesthetic Identity: "Liquid Sovereignty"
Instead of "Brutalism," we use **Liquid Sovereignty**:
1.  **Palette: The Horizon System**
    *   **Void Ground:** `#050505` (Deep, ergonomic black).
    *   **Electric Cyan:** `#00f3ff` (Clarity, Energy).
    *   **Soft Violet:** `#a78bfa` (Intelligence, Depth).

2.  **Typography Strategy (The Prestige Stack)**
    *   **Primary Display:** `Inter` (Sans-serif) — Bold, tracking `-0.04em`.
    *   **Scientific Backing:** `Cormorant Garamond` (Italic) — Used solely for "Neural Insights" or high-level summaries.
    *   **Data/Telemetry:** `JetBrains Mono` — For all IPC streams and hardware metrics.

## 2. Interactive Standards: "Digital Mercury"
1.  **The "Live Orb":** Instead of a static icon, the Lumina Orb is a dynamic, fluid element that reacts to touch and system load. It feels like "Digital Mercury."
2.  **Magnetic Attraction:** Overlays and buttons don't just hover; they subtly "lean" toward the finger (or cursor), mimicking high-end haptic quality.
3.  **Reveal Logic:** Elements seamlessly unveil using vertical clip-path masks, suggesting lines of logic being rendered in real-time.

## 3. Visual Depth & "Lunar Frost" Glass
- **Morphic Glass:** `backdrop-filter: blur(40px) saturate(200%)`.
- **Surface Texture:** A subtle 0.04% grain overlay is required to prevent "digital flatness."
- **Z-Index Strategy:**
  - `z: -10` — The Global Metadata Canvas.
  - `z: 10` — Glass Cards (System Modules).
  - `z: 100` — The Resonance Cursor (Electric Cyan glow).

## 4. Psychology: "Security is a Luxury"
*   **Minimalism is Power:** Only show what is necessary. Complexity is hidden behind the "Deep View."
*   **Tactile Reassurance:** Every action triggers a subtle visual "echo" (Glow/Bloom).
*   **Symmetry & Balance:** Layouts follow a strict mathematical grid to induce a sense of calm and control.
