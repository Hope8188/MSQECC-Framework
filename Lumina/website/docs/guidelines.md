# Lumina Website: The Gateway Guidelines (Prestige Minimalism)

## 0. The Mission: The Digital Threshold
The **Lumina Horizon Website** is not a landing page—it is a **Gateway**. It is the bridge between the telemetry-heavy world of legacy OSs (Windows/Android) and the sovereign reality of **Lumina Horizon**. It must be the most premium personal website on the internet.

## 1. Aesthetic Identity: "Prestige Minimalism"
Learning from the MSQECC "Liquid Lab" but elevating it to a high-end luxury brand (Think: Apple + High-End Automotive + Private Military).

1.  **Palette: The Deep Horizon**
    *   **Void Ground:** `#030303` (Slightly deeper than the app background).
    *   **Electric Cyan High-Contrast:** `#00f3ff` (Used for "Discovery" elements).
    *   **Lunar White:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.95)` (For text, never pure white).
    *   **Glow Accents:** `rgba(0, 243, 255, 0.15)` (Soft, broad glows).

2.  **Typography: The Command Font**
    *   **Hero Headers:** `Inter` (Extra Bold, `-0.06em` tracking, uppercase).
    *   **Scientific Backing:** `Cormorant Garamond` (Italic, for quotes on privacy).
    *   **System Telemetry:** `JetBrains Mono` (Only for raw data blocks).

## 2. Interactive Standards: "High-Agency Gravity"
1.  **The Horizon Reveal:** Elements do not "fade" or "slide." They **Coalesce**. Transitions use `clip-path` masks with momentum-based timing.
2.  **Magnetic Navigation:** Links have a large (60px) "Magnetic Reach" that pulls the custom cursor toward them.
3.  **Digital Mercury Scroll:** Page scrolling must feel "oiled." Use `Lenis` with a specific friction to mimic a high-end physical machine.

## 3. Interaction Components (Learning from MSQECC)
*   **The Syndrome Scramble:** On hover, critical text should momentarily scramble into syndrome code characters (MSQECC Logic) before resolving into the translation.
*   **The Horizon Orb:** A 3D WebGL center-piece (Torus/Sphere) that rotates based on scroll speed.
*   **Proof Modules:** Glass cards (40px radius) showing the "Privacy Parity" between Horizon and Legacy OSs.

## 4. Psychology: "The Sovereign Invite"
*   **Exclusive, Not Invasive:** No cookies banners, no tracking, no newsletter popups.
*   **Technical Supremacy:** Show the Rust code/Kernel logic snippets in `JetBrains Mono` to prove Ring-0 sovereignty.
*   **Security as Luxury:** Use wide spacing (whitespace) to communicate "Breathe-able" privacy.
